# Menty | Depression Detection

A voice-based depression detection app that listens to you talk and tells you if you might be experiencing symptoms of depression. Think of it as a digital mood ring that actually works.

## What It Does

You talk, it listens. It analyzes your voice patterns and content to detect potential signs of depression. Think of it as a digital friend who's always there to listen (but won't judge you for talking to your plants).

## How to Use

1. Open the app
2. Click the microphone button
3. Talk about your feelings (or what you had for breakfast)
4. Get your depression risk analysis
5. Repeat until you feel better or get tired of talking

## Tech Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI (because we like our APIs fast and our Python clean)
- **Frontend**: Tailwind CSS, Alpine JS, and HTMX (the holy trinity of modern web dev)
- **AI/ML**: Powered by Groq
  - Transcription: Whisper large v3 turbo (hears you even when you mumble)
  - LLM: gpt-os-20b (smarter than your average chatbot)
